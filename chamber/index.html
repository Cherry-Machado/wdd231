Events

  • SIM São Paulo 2025 - The largest music and creative economy conference in Latin America, featuring over 300 shows, 100 panels, and workshops. Dates: February 17-21, 2025. Location: Memorial da América Latina. More Info
  • Love Love A Festa - A vibrant music event featuring Gilsons, Os Garotin, and Jota.Pe, celebrating Latin American rhythms. Date: February 14, 2025. Location: Espaço Unimed, Barra Funda.
  • Ano Novo Chinês no Bairro da Liberdade - Celebrate the Chinese New Year with lion dances, traditional music, and cultural activities. Dates: February 15-16, 2025. Location: Praça da Liberdade.
  • Visita à Fábrica de Chocolate Cacau Show - A guided tour of one of São Paulo's most famous chocolate factories, including tastings and a workshop. Date: February 18, 2025. Location: Itapevi, São Paulo.
  • Feira do Pescado e Frutos do Mar - A gastronomic festival featuring over 50 seafood dishes, including paella and grilled shrimp. Dates: February 14-16, 2025. Location: Ceagesp, Vila Leopoldina.
  • Virada Cultural 2025 - A 24-hour cultural festival with music, theater, and art performances across the city. Dates: May 24-25, 2025. Location: Various venues in São Paulo.
